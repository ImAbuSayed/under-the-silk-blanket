[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the fire when your skin meets mine
We leave the shadows of the world behind
Your breath is heavy like a midnight breeze
You got me falling right upon my knees

[Verse 1]
The embers glow inside this quiet room
I trace the outlines of your sweet perfume
A whispered promise in the fading light
I want to hold you through the turning night

[Pre-Chorus]
And every time you look at me that way
There is nothing left for my poor heart to say
The temperature is rising in the dark
I feel the rhythm beating in your heart

[Chorus]
Under the silk blanket, we hide away
Drawing the curtains on the break of day
Letting my fingers memorize your face
Losing ourselves inside this warm embrace

[Post-Chorus]
(Under the silk, under the silk)
Losing ourselves inside this warm embrace

[Verse 2]
